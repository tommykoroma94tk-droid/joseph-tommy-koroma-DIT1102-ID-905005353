About The Agency

Empowering Sierra Leone's football talent and connecting them with opportunities worldwide.

Our Story

Sierra Leone Football Agency was founded with a singular mission: to discover, nurture, and showcase the incredible football talent that exists within Sierra Leone. We recognized that many talented young players lacked access to professional training, international exposure, and pathways to successful careers.

Since our establishment, we have built a comprehensive program that combines world-class coaching, mentorship, and international connections. Our experienced team works directly with players, providing them with the technical skills, tactical knowledge, and professional guidance needed to compete at the highest levels.

Today, we proudly serve as a bridge between Sierra Leone's rising football stars and professional opportunities across Africa, Europe, and beyond. Our success is measured not just in contracts signed, but in the dreams realized and futures transformed.

Our Achievements

50+ Professional Placements

Successfully placed over 50 talented players with professional clubs across Africa and Europe.

International Partnerships

Established strong partnerships with top clubs, scouts, and agencies in 15+ countries worldwide.

Youth Development Excellence

Trained over 200 young athletes through our comprehensive development programs since 2018.
